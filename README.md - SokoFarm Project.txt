SokoFarm Project.
This projectis aimed at helping farmers minimize losses made by their produce going bad due to inadequate market. It is also designed to assit consumers and or wholesellers 
...having the farmers and end users interests at heart, to minimize losses obtained from buying farm produce directly from the farmers.
Its a WebApp (which will also include a mobile app)designed to enable farmers get the best out of their farming by selling their produce direclty to the end consumers. 
These farmers will also be able to make decisions on who to sell thei rproduce to based on what the client is willing to offer. 
The consumer or user will also be able to save more since s/he will be able to obtained fresh farm produce directly from the farmers.
